App Name: One Goal

Short Description:
A minimal goal tracker that helps you focus on achieving one single goal every day.

Full Description:
One Goal is a simple, no-nonsense app designed to help you focus on what truly matters: a single goal.

In a world full of complex task lists and overwhelming productivity systems, One Goal gives you a clean slate. You set one goal, and each day you answer a simple question: Did you do it today?

Features:
- Set and display your one primary goal in a large, distraction-free environment.
- Tap "Done Today" or "Not Today" to track your daily progress.
- Build powerful streaks to motivate you and track your longest winning streak.
- Minimalist, premium design with Dark Mode support for a relaxing experience.
- Completely private: Everything is stored locally on your device. No accounts, no internet required, and zero data collection.

Stay focused, maintain your streak, and accomplish your One Goal.
